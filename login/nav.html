Exhibitions

Academy of Fine Arts, Kolkata

Established in 1933, the Academy of Fine Arts in Kolkata is one of the most significant art galleries in India. Various famous paintings by eminent Indian and foreign artists are displayed here.

Government Museum, Chennai

Also known as the Madras Museum, the Government Museum in Chennai is the second oldest museum and art gallery in India. Established in the year 1851, it witnesses a heavy footfall of visitors each year.

Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai

Established in 1952, Jehangir Art Gallery in Mumbai is owned by a private organization and has paintings of almost all famous Indian artists from Jamini Roy to Arpita Singh.
